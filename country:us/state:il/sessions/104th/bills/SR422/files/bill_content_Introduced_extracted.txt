(Tirelli) Caparelli, who 4passed away on August 29, 2025; and 5    WHEREAS, Assunta Caparelli was born to Carmella and 6Salvatore Tirelli in Naples, Italy on August 31, 1947; and 7    WHEREAS, Assunta Caparelli worked in the restaurant 8industry for 30 years, initially dedicating herself as a 9server for two decades before becoming a chef, through which 10she infused her Italian heritage with her love of food; during 11her career, she was noted for her impeccable work ethic and 12culinary passion; and 13    WHEREAS, Assunta Caparelli enjoyed tending to her flower 14gardens and supporting the San Francisco 49ers, especially 15quarterback Joe Montana; and 16    WHEREAS, Assunta Caparelli was a woman of strong faith, 17finding joy and solace in the traditions and community of the 18Catholic church; she was known for speaking her mind, and she 19infused the warmth and liveliness of her Italian heritage in 20everything she did; she will be remembered for her authentic 21spirit and her passion for life; and   SR0422- 2 -LRB104 15258 ECR 28408 r1    WHEREAS, Assunta Caparelli was the mother of Lisa (Tom) 2Caparelli-Ruff; the grandmother of Nicolas (Julianna) Ruff and 3Luke Ruff; the sister of Pasquale, Consglia (Bob), Anna Maria, 4Rita, Pasqualina, Patrizia, Rosaria, and the late Maria; and 5the aunt of many; therefore, be it 6    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 7ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we mourn the passing of 8Assunta L. (Tirelli) Caparelli and extend our sincere 9condolences to her family, friends, and all who knew and loved 10her; and be it further 11    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 12presented to the family of Assunta Caparelli as an expression 13of our deepest sympathy.
