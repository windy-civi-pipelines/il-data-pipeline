Full Text of HB4162 ILGA.GOV HB4162 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB4162 Introduced 10/15/2025, by Rep. Martin McLaughlin SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act    Creates the Official Speaker of the House of Representatives Portrait Act. Specifies that portraits of the Speakers of the House of Representatives of Illinois, painted or otherwise created as visual images, displayed in the State Capitol shall be the official State portraits of the Speakers of the House of Representatives of Illinois. Prohibits the use of State funds to finance or pay for a portrait or other image of any person who has served as Speaker of the House of Representatives of Illinois and who has been convicted of a felony relating to or arising out of or in connection with his or her service as an elected official.LRB104 15768 JDS 28956 b  A BILL FOR HB4162LRB104 15768 JDS 28956 b1    AN ACT concerning government. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 1. The portraits of the Speakers of the 8House of Representatives of Illinois, painted or otherwise 9created as visual images, displayed in the State Capitol shall 10be the official State portraits of the Speakers of the House of 11Representatives of Illinois. 12    Section 10. State funding prohibited for felons.
