SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Frank William Rasmusson of Crest Hill, 
4who passed away on September 22, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Frank Rasmusson was born to Gladys (Smith) Buck 
6and Frank Buck in Joliet on September 11, 1943, and he was 
7later adopted by Martel Rasmusson; as a child, he took classes 
8at the Joliet Amateur Radio Society and earned his FCC 
9license, allowing him to communicate through Morse code with 
10call sign KA9VPH; he graduated from Joliet Township High 
11School in 1962; he served as a specialist E-5 in the United 
12States Army from 1964 to 1967; during his military service, he 
13was an electronics instructor at Fort Gordon; he was honorably 
14discharged from the U.S. Army Reserve in 1969; and
 
15    WHEREAS, Frank Rasmusson worked for the Illinois Bell 
16Telephone Company; while employed by the Illinois Bell 
17Telephone Company, he opened Frank's Electronics on Jefferson 
18Street in Joliet; after his retirement, he continued to fix 
19radios and more from his home in Crest Hill; and
 
20    WHEREAS, Frank Rasmusson served as a precinct committeeman 
21for Lockport Township Precinct 21 and worked on dozens of 
22campaigns for the Democratic Party, helping many candidates
