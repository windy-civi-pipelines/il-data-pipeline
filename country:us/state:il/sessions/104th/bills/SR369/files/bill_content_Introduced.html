SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Robert L. "Bob" Harder of Altamont, 
4who passed away on June 8, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Bob Harder, the son of Casey and Madonna (Brandt) 
6Harder, was born in Shumway on August 30, 1937; he graduated 
7from Altamont Community High School in 1955 and Eastern 
8Illinois University in 1959, where he was a member of the 
9university's track team; he married Shirley Galloway at 
10Altamont First United Methodist Church on August 18, 1957, and 
11they had three children; and
 
12    WHEREAS, Bob Harder was a retired junior high school 
13teacher who previously taught at schools in Toledo and 
14Altamont; he also served as a clerk for the Francis Auction 
15Company, assisted at Gieseking Funeral Home, and owned The 
16Mowing Company; and
 
17    WHEREAS, Bob Harder was a member of Altamont First United 
18Methodist Church and a former member of the Altamont Volunteer 
19Fire Department and the Effingham County Fair Board; for his 
20commitment to serving his community, he was named Altamont's 
21Citizen of the Year in 2003; and
