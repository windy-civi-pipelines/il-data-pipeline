SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Beauty R. Kirk, who passed away on 
4August 29, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Beauty Kirk was born on February 6, 1953; she 
6received an Associate in Applied Science in Paralegal Studies 
7from Harper College in Palatine and a Bachelor of Science in 
8Business Administration with a Sales & Marketing concentration 
9from DeVry University in Chicago in December 2013; she then 
10attended Keller Graduate School of Management in Chicago, 
11where she earned a Master's in Public Administration (MPA) 
12with a Non-Profit concentration in December 2015, a graduate 
13certificate in wireless communications in February 2017, a 
14Master's in Information Systems Management (MISM) in April 
152018, and a graduate certificate in information security in 
16October 2018; she next studied through Northcentral University 
17of Phoenix, Arizona, where she obtained an Education 
18Specialist (Ed.S.) degree in February 2021 and a Certificate 
19of Advanced Graduate Study in Business Specialist (CAGSBUSD) 
20in May 2023; and
 
21    WHEREAS, Beauty Kirk played an integral part of the growth 
22and development of Employee Connections' programs and services 
23that were offered to the youth and young adults throughout
