Full Text of HB4111 ILGA.GOV HB4111 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB4111 Introduced 10/15/2025, by Rep. Harry Benton SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 110 ILCS 167/25 new    Amends the Public Higher Education Act. Requires each public institution of higher education in the State to adopt a policy that authorizes a student to withdraw from the public institution of higher education under extenuating circumstances, which shall include a provision to refund a student for tuition and fees and room and board costs paid by the student or the student's family, to be prorated, for the semester during which the student withdraws.
