4success with the 1960 release of his breakthrough comedy

6    WHEREAS, Bob Newhart was a native of Chicago's Austin

9of Chicago's St. Ignatius High School and Loyola University

16competition, including records by some of the 20th century's

18Harry Belafonte; and
