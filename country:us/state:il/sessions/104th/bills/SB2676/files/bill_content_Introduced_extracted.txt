Full Text of SB2676 ILGA.GOV SB2676 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB2676 Introduced 10/14/2025, by Sen. Willie Preston SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 105 ILCS 5/27-20.7    Amends the School Code. Provides that, beginning with the 2026-2027 school year, the cursive writing instruction offered by public elementary schools must be offered to students in grades 3 through 5 (rather than requiring school districts to determine at what grade level or levels students are to be offered cursive writing, provided that such instruction must be offered before students complete grade 5).LRB104 14343 LNS 27476 bSTATE MANDATES ACT MAY REQUIRE REIMBURSEMENTMAY APPLY A BILL FOR SB2676LRB104 14343 LNS 27476 b1    AN ACT concerning education. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5.
