and 16    WHEREAS, Morgan Dubiel served on the Niles Planning and 17Zoning Board for 13 years, where he was finance chairman for 18more than two years, and he served as a Niles village trustee 19since April 1, 2023; and 20    WHEREAS, Morgan Dubiel was a founding member of Niles   HR0489- 2 -LRB104 15536 LAW 28701 r1Crime Stoppers; and 2    WHEREAS, Morgan Dubiel enjoyed exploring and hiking 3through the woods and the North Branch of the Chicago River 4with his family; and 5    WHEREAS, Morgan Dubiel was a faithful member of the Church 6of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints, serving as a stake 7president and helping oversee church programs in several 8individual congregations, and his love of service was 9demonstrated through his dedication to his family and to the 10Village of Niles; and 11    WHEREAS, Morgan Dubiel is survived by his wife, Leslie, 12and his children, Logan, Arden, and Caleb; therefore, be it 13    RESOLVED, BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE 14HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that 15we mourn the passing of Morgan Dubiel and extend our sincere 16condolences to his family, friends, and all who knew and loved 17him; and be it further 18    RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 19presented to the family of Morgan Dubiel as an expression of 20our deepest sympathy.
