and 18    WHEREAS, Gary Thomas loved playing cards and listening to 19music; he was an avid outdoorsman who had a passion for hunting 20and fishing, including spending several years commercial 21fishing, running hoop nets, trotlines, and trammel nets, and   SR0379- 2 -LRB104 14333 ECR 27466 r1enjoying many summer nights catfishing on the Illinois River 2with his family; and 3    WHEREAS, Gary Thomas will be remembered for living life on 4his own terms, making every minute count; and 5    WHEREAS, Gary Thomas was preceded in death by his parents 6and his hunting companions, Cinder and Lucy; and 7    WHEREAS, Gary Thomas is survived by his wife of 57 years, 8Alberta; his daughter, Teresa Lynn (Thomas) Donley; and his 9grandson, Patrick Ryan Donley; therefore, be it 10    RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTH GENERAL 11ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we mourn the passing of 12Gary Wayne Thomas and extend our sincere condolences to his 13family, friends, and all who knew and loved him;
