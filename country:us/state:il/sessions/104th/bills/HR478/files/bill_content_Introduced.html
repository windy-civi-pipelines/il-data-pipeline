HOUSE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois House of 
3Representatives are saddened to learn of the death of Danise 
4Susanne Habun of Elgin, who passed away on August 16, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Danise Habun was born to Daniel and Susanne 
6(Maday) Habun in Milwaukee, Wisconsin on September 3, 1956; 
7she was raised in South Milwaukee, where she attended St. 
8John's Grade School; she graduated from Notre Dame High School 
9in Milwaukee in 1974; she received a degree in psychology with 
10an emphasis on women's studies from the University of 
11Wisconsin-Platteville in 1980; after college, she moved to 
12Illinois, where she eventually settled in Elgin; and
 
13    WHEREAS, Danise Habun worked as a social worker, 
14dedicating her time and energy to being a voice for those who 
15were silenced and to fight for all those who lived in her 
16community; she also worked as a legal advocate for the 
17Community Crisis Center in Elgin and with the HIV community at 
18Open Door until the time of her passing; and
 
19    WHEREAS, Danise Habun organized the Dr. Martin Luther King 
20Jr. Day Prayer Breakfast celebration in Elgin every year; she 
21served as executive director of Hanover Township's Mental 
22Health Board, and she fought to establish a similar board in
