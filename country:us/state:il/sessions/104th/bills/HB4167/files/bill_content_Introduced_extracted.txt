Full Text of HB4167 ILGA.GOV HB4167 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026HB4167 Introduced , by Rep. Kevin Schmidt SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 50 ILCS 105/4b new    Amends the Public Officer Prohibited Activities Act. Provides that beginning on the effective date of the amendatory Act, an executive official of a municipality may not hold any office or employment with a township if there is a contractual relationship or pecuniary interest between the municipality and the township during the executive official's term of office. Provides that "executive official" includes, but is not limited to (1) the mayor of a city; (2) a member of the board of alderpersons, or similar body, of a city; (3) the president of a village or unincorporated town; (4) a member of the board of trustees, or similar body, of a village or unincorporated town; or (5) a commissioner of a municipality under the commission form of municipal government.LRB104 15636 RTM 28803 b  A BILL FOR HB4167LRB104 15636 RTM 28803 b1    AN ACT concerning local government. 2    Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3represented in the General Assembly: 4    Section 5.
