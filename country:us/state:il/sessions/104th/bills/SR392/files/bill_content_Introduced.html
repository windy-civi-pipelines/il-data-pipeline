SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Bernard J. "Bernie" Goitein, Ph.D., 
4who passed away on August 2, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Dr. Goitein, the son of Dr. Lionel Goitein and 
6Rachael Hahn Goitein-Galperin, in Queens, New York City, New 
7York on July 7, 1950; he attended New York schools before 
8moving with his family to Israel, where he graduated from 
9Hebrew University High School; he received his bachelor's and 
10master's degrees in psychology from Hebrew University; he 
11returned to the United States in 1975; he received his Ph.D. in 
12Psychology from the University of Michigan (U-M) in 1981; 
13during his doctoral studies, he was an assistant study 
14director at U-M's Survey Research Center under the Survey 
15Research Institute (SRI), where he worked on national Quality 
16of Employment Surveys; and
 
17    WHEREAS, Dr. Goitein taught business management at Bradley 
18University, where he also served as director of the Center for 
19Business and Economic Research; for many years, his 
20often-quoted Consumer Confidence surveys reliably measured the 
21health of central Illinois' economy with economic indicators 
22that accurately predicted fluctuations in metro-Peoria's 
23prosperity; during his career, he authored numerous
