SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Thomas William "Tom" Ritter of Blue 
4Mound, who passed away on August 20, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Tom Ritter, the son of William H. and Arlone 
6(Deardorff) Ritter, in Decatur on February 12, 1952; he 
7graduated from Blue Mound High School; he received a Bachelor 
8of Science in Agriculture from the University of Illinois 
9Urbana-Champaign; he married Diana D. Virt in 1979; and
 
10    WHEREAS, Tom Ritter worked in the agricultural industry; 
11he served as president of the Macon County Farm Bureau and as 
12one of the longest tenured Illinois Farm Bureau Crop Watchers, 
13reporting weekly in Macon County; he was also a member of the 
14Macon County 4-H Foundation, the Meridian Ag Foundation, the 
15Illinois Soybean Association, the National Soybean Checkoff 
16Board, and the U.S. Grains Council; and
 
17    WHEREAS, Tom Ritter served as a trustee, an elder, a 
18deacon, and a liturgist at Central Christian Church in 
19Decatur; he also served as a former president and trustee of 
20Richland Community College, a liaison to the Illinois 
21Community College Trustees Association, president of the 
22Meridian School Board and the Blue Mound School Board, and
