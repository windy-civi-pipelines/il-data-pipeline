SENATE RESOLUTION

 
2    WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened 
3to learn of the death of Rosemary S. Argus of Des Plaines, who 
4passed away on June 18, 2025; and
 
5    WHEREAS, Rosemary Argus was born to Joseph Lewandowski and 
6Mary (Dziopa) Lewandowski in Chicago on August 29, 1936; and
 
7    WHEREAS, Rosemary Argus served as a commissioner of the 
8Mount Prospect Park District Board from 1974 to 2001, where 
9she was the board's first female commissioner and served as 
10its first female president from 1987 to 1993; during her 
11tenure, she supported providing school children with hot 
12lunches and creating before and after-school child care 
13programs; she then served as an alderperson on the Des Plaines 
14City Council, representing the 8th Ward, from 2001 to 2011, 
15and she supported the Des Plaines Chamber of Commerce; in 
16addition to her tireless service to the communities of Mount 
17Prospect and Des Plaines, she also worked full-time at United 
18Airlines for over 20 years; and
 
19    WHEREAS, Rosemary Argus served as executive director of 
20the Des Plaines Community Foundation beginning in the 1990s, 
21which delivers Thanksgiving meals to families in need, donates 
22wheelchair ramps to people with disabilities, offers college
