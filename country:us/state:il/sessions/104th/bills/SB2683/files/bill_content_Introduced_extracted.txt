Full Text of SB2683 ILGA.GOV SB2683 - 104th General Assembly 104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY State of Illinois 2025 and 2026SB2683 Introduced 10/14/2025, by Sen. Chris Balkema SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 30 ILCS 350/17.5    Amends the Local Government Debt Reform Act. Provides that, in the case of bonds authorized to be issued under the School Code and approved by voters of Iroquois County Community Unit School District Number 9 in an April 2021 referendum, the bond approval, once obtained, remains for 10 years after the date of the referendum.
